### 🎯 MASTER PROMPT (восстановление и осовременивание фото)

```
Fully restore and enhance an old, damaged, low-resolution photograph and transform it into a clean, modern, high-quality image.

CRITICAL: Strictly preserve the person's real identity, facial structure, expression, age, and proportions. Do NOT alter the face, do NOT beautify, do NOT invent details that are not present.

Restoration tasks:
- Remove dust, scratches, stains, film grain, noise, and scanning artifacts
- Gently reduce blur while preserving original softness (do not oversharpen)
- Restore missing or faded details carefully, based only on existing visual information
- Improve clarity, contrast, and tonal balance naturally

Colorization (if black and white):
- Apply realistic, historically accurate colors
- Use soft, natural skin tones (avoid oversaturation)
- Keep colors muted and believable (no bright artificial colors)

Lighting and tone:
- Preserve original light direction and shadows
- Soft, natural lighting (no HDR, no dramatic artificial effects)
- Maintain authentic atmosphere of the original photo

Texture and detail:
- Keep skin natural, slightly soft if original is low detail
- Avoid artificial sharpness or plastic look
- Restore clothing texture in a simple, realistic way

Background:
- Keep original background composition
- Clean and soften it slightly, without reconstruction or invention

Final result:
- Photorealistic modern image
- High resolution (4K quality look)
- Clean, natural, cinematic photograph
- No stylization, no artistic filters

Output: one single restored realistic photo, no collage, no text, no watermarks.
```

### 🎬 MASTER PROMPT — REBUILD → TRUE B&W → MODERN COLOR (PRO)

```text
Fully restore an old, damaged photograph that may contain inaccurate or artificial colorization, and convert it into a clean, authentic modern color photograph through a strict two-step process.

STEP 1 — REMOVE OLD COLOR & CREATE TRUE BLACK AND WHITE:
- Completely ignore and remove any existing colorization
- Reconstruct a clean, neutral black-and-white image as if the photo was originally shot in monochrome
- Use natural grayscale tones only (no color contamination)
- Balanced tonal range: deep blacks, clean whites, smooth midtones
- Preserve original light, shadows, and atmosphere

ABSOLUTE PRIORITY — FACE IDENTITY PRESERVATION:
- Preserve 100% of the real person's identity
- Do NOT change facial structure, proportions, expression, or age
- Do NOT beautify or idealize the face
- Do NOT invent details (eyes, teeth, eyelashes, skin texture)
- If details are missing — keep them soft and natural
- Maintain natural asymmetry and imperfections

RESTORATION (APPLIES BEFORE COLORIZATION):
- Remove dust, scratches, stains, film grain, noise, and scanning artifacts
- Gently reduce blur without artificial sharpening
- Restore only real existing details
- Do NOT hallucinate missing features

STEP 2 — NATURAL COLORIZATION FROM CLEAN B&W:
- Apply realistic, historically and physically plausible colors
- Skin tones: soft, neutral, natural (no saturation boost)

SKIN & TEXTURE CONTROL:
- Keep skin natural, slightly soft if source is low detail

- Preserve natural tonal transitions

LIGHTING:
- Strictly preserve original light direction and shadow structure

BACKGROUND:
- Keep original composition exactly
- Clean and softly restore only
- No reconstruction, no added elements

OPTICAL REALISM:
- Natural lens rendering (no AI look)
- Subtle depth of field only if present originally
- No cinematic exaggeration

FINAL RESULT:
- Photorealistic modern color photograph
- Clean, natural, high resolution (4K look)
- Authentic, documentary-level realism
- Looks like it was captured with a modern camera, but true to the original

OUTPUT RULES:
- One single image only
```

### 💡 Важный смысл этого промпта (почему он сильный)

Ты фактически заставляешь модель:

1. **Сначала "очистить правду"** (правильный ч/б, без чужой раскраски)
2. И только потом — **строить цвет заново, как если бы фото было снято сегодня**

Это даёт намного более **реалистичный и «не-нейросетевой» результат**, чем прямое перекрашивание старого фото.
