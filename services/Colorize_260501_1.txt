 PROMPT: Colorize & Modernize Black-and-White Photo (PRO)

Task:
Transform a black-and-white photograph into a modern, high-quality, realistic color photograph.

ABSOLUTE PRIORITY — FACE IDENTITY PRESERVATION:

Preserve 100% of the real person’s identity
Do NOT change facial structure, proportions, expression, or age
Do NOT beautify, stylize, or idealize the face
Do NOT invent missing details (eyes, teeth, eyelashes, skin texture)
If details are unclear or blurred — keep them soft and natural
Maintain natural asymmetry and imperfections

COLORIZATION RULES (STRICT REALISM):

Apply natural, historically plausible colors
Skin tones must be soft, neutral, and realistic (no oversaturation)
Avoid artificial or vivid colors (no strong reds, blues, or greens)
Hair color should be muted and natural
Clothing colors should be simple, realistic, and era-appropriate
No stylization, no artistic filters, no cinematic color grading

IMAGE RESTORATION (BEFORE COLORIZATION):

Remove dust, scratches, stains, noise, film grain, and scanning artifacts
Gently reduce blur without artificial sharpening
Restore only real existing details — do NOT hallucinate new ones
Preserve original texture and softness where needed

LIGHT & TONE PRESERVATION:

Keep original lighting direction and contrast
Maintain natural tonal balance (soft highlights, clean shadows)
No HDR, no dramatic relighting
Preserve original atmosphere of the photo

FINAL OUTPUT REQUIREMENTS:

Photorealistic modern color photograph
Clean, natural, high-resolution (4K quality)
Natural depth of field (no artificial blur)
No added elements, no background reconstruction beyond original
No text, no watermark, no artistic reinterpretation
